Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Safe Return Window?

A Safe Return Window is a dedicated buffer period (usually 10-15 minutes) automatically granted when your turn is approaching. This allows you to walk back to the service location without worrying about losing your spot if the queue moves faster than expected.

How is queue predictability calculated?

Our system uses a combination of historical data from the same time/day, current staff levels, and real-time processing speeds. The "Wait Time Advisor" updates every 60 seconds to ensure the most accurate estimation possible.

Can I join multiple queues at once?

To ensure fairness for all users, most service providers limit active tickets to one per phone number. However, you can view the wait times of other nearby services at any time.
